Drone registration

There are two types of registration:

  • Recreational
  • Commercial

Recreational drone registration applies to drones or model aircraft that weigh more than 250 grams that are operated for sport, education or recreation. Drones or model aircraft that weigh 250 grams or less do not need to be registered.

Commercial drone registration applies to all drones or RPA that are operated for business or as part of your job, regardless of its weight.

More information about drone registration is available on the CASA website.

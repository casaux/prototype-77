There are two parts to your digital accreditation.

The first part is what you see inside Google Pay on your phone and contains a QR code that allows anyone to confirm your accreditation is an official CASA issued accreditation.

The second part contains the details of your accreditation as they appear on CASA's records. To access this view, follow the instructions below.

Tap the "Details" button at the bottom of your screen

Scroll down to see all the details of your accreditation.

You can add your digital accreditation to Google Pay below.

By adding your digital accreditation to Google Pay, you consent for the Civil Aviation Safety Authority to disclose the information in your digital accreditation to Google servers, which may be located outside Australia. You are also consenting to your digital accreditation being verified through the QR code on the front of the digital accreditation to anyone you show the digital accreditation to.

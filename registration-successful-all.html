Important:

  • Make sure anyone operating your registered drone holds the appropriate operator accreditation or licence.

  • To find out where you can and can't fly, download a CASA-verified drone safety app.

  • Any drone(s) recorded as ‘foreign registered’ is not registered in Australia. Instead, sthis type of drone(s) are authorised to be flown and operated in Australia. This authorisation is not a substitute for the operator to hold an operator accreditation, remote pilot licence or remotely piloted aircraft operator’s certificate.

  • Any drone without a serial number must have a CASA-generted registration mark affixed to it. Tips about how to do this are on the CASA website. Your CASA-generated registration mark can be found on your Certificate of Registration or from the Manage registered drones section of myCASA.

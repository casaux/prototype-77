Digital accreditation

Close

There are two parts to your digital accreditation.

The first part is what you see inside your Apple Wallet on your iPhone and contains a QR code that allows anyone to confirm your accreditation is an official CASA issued accreditation.

The second part contains the details of your accreditation as they appears on CASA's records. To access this view, follow the instructions below.

Tap the 3 dots at the top right of your screen

Scroll down to see all the details of your accreditation.

You can add your digital accreditation to your Apple Wallet below.

By adding your digital accreditation to your Apple Wallet, you are consenting to your digital accreditation being confirmed through the QR code on the front of the digital accreditation to anyone you show the digital accreditation to.

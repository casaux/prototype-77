Drone registration

Back

There are two types of drone registration:

  • Recreational drone registration
  • Commercial drone registration

Recreational drone registration is for drones or RPA that weigh 250g or above and that are going to be operated for sport, educational or recreational use only. Drones or RPA that weigh less than 250g do not require recreational drone registration.

Commercial drone registration is required for drones or RPA that are going to be operated for business or as part of your job. Regardless of their weight.

If you are unsure what kind of registration your drones or RPA require, more information can be found on the CASA website.
